Cheese Lasagna

This lasagna recipe is an easy vegetarian lasagna with ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheeses.

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • ricotta cheese
  • eggs
  • grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and pepper
  • olive oil
  • garlic, minced
  • spaghetti sauce
  • Italian seasoning
  • shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook for 8 to 10 minutes; drain and lay noodles flat on foil to cool.
  3. Mix ricotta, eggs, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper together in a bowl; mix well.
  4. Heat oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat; add garlic and saut√© 2 minutes. Stir in spaghetti sauce and Italian seasoning until warmed through, stirring occasionally, 2 to 5 minutes.
  5. Spread 1/2 cup of sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Cover with a layer of noodles. Spread 1/2 of the ricotta mixture over noodles; top with another layer of noodles. Pour 1 1/2 cups of sauce over noodles and spread the remaining ricotta over the sauce. Top with remaining noodles and sauce; sprinkle with remaining mozzarella. Cover with a greased sheet of foil.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, about 45 minutes.
